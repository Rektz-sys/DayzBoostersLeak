Why did I create DayZ BoosterZ

We strive to help you automate building your server

We are firm believers in giving back and breaking down barriers because excellence should never be behind a paywall. DayZ BoosterZ was born out of a passion for DayZ and a desire to make the process of setting up and maintaining servers more accessible to everyone.

With our tools, you can quickly and easily modify your servers, freeing up your time to focus on what you love most – playing DayZ.

Our mission is to provide robust, user-friendly tools that empower gamers of all levels to create and manage their own DayZ servers. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, DayZ BoosterZ has the resources and support you need to succeed.

Whether you're looking to enhance loot spawns, optimize the in-game economy, or customize the perfect loadout, we have everything you need (almost).

Play DayZ, Not Configuration Wars.

Streamline your server modifications with a user-friendly platform designed to save time and focus on what truly matters—playing DayZ.
