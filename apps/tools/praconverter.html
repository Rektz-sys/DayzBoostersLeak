PRA Converter Guide
Quick Overview

Convert gift box coordinates into Player Restricted Areas (PRA) and safe positions with this simple tool. Perfect for game developers looking to streamline their level setup process.

How to Use
1
Prepare Your Gift Boxes
PRA Boxes GiftBox_Small_1
Safe Positions GiftBox_Small_2

Place boxes in pairs - each PRA box needs a corresponding safe position.

2
Upload Your JSON

Drag and drop your JSON file into the upload area, or click to select it from your computer.

3
Review the Output

The converter will generate:

  • PRABoxes: Restricted area definitions
  • safePositions3D: Safe teleport locations
4
Save Your Work

Use the copy or download buttons to save your converted coordinates.

Pro Tips
  • Double-check gift box names for exact spelling and capitalization
  • Ensure you have an equal number of PRA boxes and safe positions
  • Watch for validation messages if the conversion doesn't work as expected
